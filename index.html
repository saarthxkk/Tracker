Daily Routine

The Study Ritual

Establishing a daily routine is key to deep focus and rewiring your logical intuition. Follow this structure every day.

Morning Focus

  • Review previous day's mistakes 15 mins
  • Watch theory video & take brief notes 45 mins
  • Dry run the algorithm on paper 30 mins

Evening Coding

  • Solve easy/medium problems on LeetCode 90 mins
  • Analyze optimal time/space complexity 20 mins
  • Document mistakes in self-made log 15 mins

Final Advice

Don’t try to complete all questions fast. Focus on quality rather than quantity.

Your target should be:

  • Easy: Solve within 15-20 mins without looking at hints.
  • Medium: Dry run properly on paper before coding. Focus on the core pattern.
  • Hard: Understand the underlying pattern first, build a sub-optimal solution, then study the optimal one. Retry solving it 3 days later.

For each incorrect submission or problem you struggled with, document: Mistake, Pattern, Correct Logic, and set a Revision Date in your personal diary. Spaced repetition is the secret to mastery.
